PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: September 2020

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Fundun Games (also referred to as “Fundun,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, stores, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game applications via Google Play. Our mobile games, as controlled by Google Play, are open to those aged 13 and older who reside within the United States.

Information Collected

Fundun Games does not collect, store, or use personal data, log data, or game usage data.

Personal Data is information that identifies users, including but not limited to email address, phone number, and residential address, city, state. We do not collect personal data either by directly asking users to provide personal data or by automatically collecting personal data.

Log Data is information automatically collected, including but not limited to mobile device information, IP address, cookies or tracking information, and network connection. We do not collect log data.

Gameplay and Service Usage Data is information related to using our games, including but not limited to player name, game progress, or score tracking. We do not collect gameplay or service usage data.

Children Under Age 13

The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (“COPPA”) protects children under the age of 13 from personalized advertising. Our games are only open to users age 13 and older via Google Play.

California and EU Residents

The California Consumer Privacy Act (“CCPA”) provides California residents with specific rights regarding their personal information. Fundun Games does not collect personal information.

The California Online Privacy Protection Act (“CalOppa”) is enforceable for anyone who collects personal information from California residents and requires communication of which types of information is collected. Fundun Games does not collect personal information.

The General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) has specialized requirements to personalized information for those residing within the European Union (“EU”). Fundun Games is only open to those residing within the United States, via Google Play.

Personalized Advertisements

We use “AdMob by Google,” which allows Google to network to their third-party advertisers and personalize advertisements that are displayed at the bottom of our mobile games.

Google and their third-party advertisers may collect personal information.
